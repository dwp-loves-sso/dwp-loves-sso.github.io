Challenges - 3D Master Palms

You would need a new algorithm that can first convert a 3D master palm image into a 2D print comparable to a latent for Rank One.

You would also need a new algorithm that can take a single palm print and identify previously unknown ridges and refine the master image, like an aggregate of all palm prints.

Will the 3D palm ever be admissible in court? Because if not, the most we can hope for is the 3D palm can serve as a quick search, where a practitioner can click the 10+ minutiae points he wants and the algorithm returns the original palm print card from which those minutiae were aggregated.
